Galeri wisata polandia

Warsawa market

Warsaw Market being one of the tourist attractions in Poland that is a pity to miss, especially if you like shopping trips. Warsaw Market is located in the city of Warsaw and is one of the tourist destinations that quite attract the attention of tourists.

Warsaw-rising-museum

Warsaw Rising Museum Still in the city of Warsaw, there is one of the other interesting Polish attractions that you must visit, namely the Warsaw Rising Museum. The museum is dedicated to the Warsaw rebels of 1944 during which the Polish Underground resistance fought the Germans. The uprising lasted 63 days and many casualties fell from both sides.

lazienki-park

Lazienki Park, A tourist attraction in Poland that you must visit next is Lazienki Park or Lazienki Park. The 76-hectare park is located in the center of Warsaw and is one of the largest urban parks in Poland.

gdansk-old-town

Dgansk Old Town, Dgansk Old is also one of the tourist attractions in Poland that you should visit. Tourism of this old town is one of the areas that has an important role in the history of the 20th century."

wieliczka-salt-mine-tunnels

"Wieliczka Salt Mine Tunnels, Wieliczka The next tourist attraction in Poland that you should not miss is the Wielizcka Salt Mine Tunnels. True to its name, it is a salt mine operating since the 13th century. This salt mine became one of the oldest salt mines in the world that can still be visited today".

krakow-cloth-hall

"Krakow Cloth Hall, Krakow Still in Krakow City, there is one of the attractions in Poland that is no less interesting to visit, namely Krakow Cloth Hall. It is a hall or square that dates back to the Renaissance and has always been the center of international trade in Poland.".

market-square-krakow

"Main Market Square Krakow, Krakow This square is indeed a tourist attraction not only for local tourists but also foreign tourists. Because, this square is surrounded by several historic townhouses. The rows of historic buildings surrounding the square are indeed very interesting to visit.".

vistula-river

"Vistula River, Krakow The Vistula River is one of the tourist attractions that is visited by quite a lot of tourists during the summer in Poland. The riverbank is filled with green grass and is the best spot for local people to lie down and have a relaxing picnic with family or friends. Not only that, you can also go along the largest river in Poland by boat while enjoying the beautiful view of the city of Krakow".
